About Us

CHS Competitive Programming is a club centered around hands-on problem solving. Throughout the year, club members will learn various algorithms and practice implementing them by solving real competitive programming problems. With the help of our qualified mentors, club members will prepare for coding contests such as USACO (USA Computing Olympiad) and other prestigious online competitions. This year, our club is also branching out to competitive cybersecurity and CTFs. Join us and boost your critical thinking and coding skills!
